# Go-To-Market Plan

## 1) Who are you selling to?
- Small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs) looking to improve customer support.
- Customer support teams within these businesses.
- Business owners who prioritize customer satisfaction and feedback.

## 2) What are you offering them?
- An automated feedback processing tool that analyzes client feedback forms.
- A prioritized list of improvement tasks for support teams based on feedback.
- Insights and recommendations to enhance customer support quality.

## 3) How will they hear about it? (Free channels only)
- Social media posts on platforms like LinkedIn, Twitter, and Facebook.
- Blog articles discussing the importance of customer feedback and support.
- Guest posts on industry-related websites and forums.
- Email newsletters targeting SMBs and support professionals.
- Word-of-mouth referrals from existing users.

## 4) How will they try it?
- Offer a free trial or a freemium version of the tool.
- Provide a demo or walkthrough video on the website.
- Host webinars showcasing the tool’s features and benefits.
- Create a simple onboarding process with tutorials and FAQs.

## 5) How will they pay you?
- Subscription-based model with monthly or annual payment options.
- Tiered pricing based on the number of users or features accessed.
- Option for one-time payments for specific features or add-ons.
