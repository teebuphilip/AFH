### 1) Who are you selling to?
- Accountants and accounting firms
- Small to medium-sized businesses needing bookkeeping support
- Freelancers and independent contractors managing their own finances

### 2) What are you offering them?
- An automated tool that generates monthly financial summaries from raw bookkeeping data
- User-friendly interface for easy data input and report generation
- Time-saving solution that reduces manual bookkeeping efforts

### 3) How will they hear about it? (Free channels only)
- Social media posts on platforms like LinkedIn and Twitter targeting accountants
- Blog articles and guest posts on accounting websites discussing the benefits of automation
- Free webinars or online workshops demonstrating the tool's features
- Engaging in relevant online forums and communities (e.g., Reddit, accounting groups on Facebook)

### 4) How will they try it?
- Offer a free trial period (e.g., 14 days) to test the tool
- Provide a demo version with limited features for hands-on experience
- Create tutorial videos and guides to help users navigate the tool during the trial

### 5) How will they pay you?
- Subscription model with monthly or annual payment options
- Tiered pricing based on the number of clients or reports generated
- Option for one-time payments for specific features or reports
