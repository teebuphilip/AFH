# GTM One-Pager — Contract Renewal Reminders (No Paid Ads)

## 1) Who are you selling to?
- Small B2B sales teams (5–50 reps)
- Sales ops managers or founders who track renewals in spreadsheets/CRM

## 2) What are you offering them?
- A simple system that stores contract renewal dates and sends automatic reminders before renewal
- Clear value: reduces churn and missed renewals
- Original KEEP file: `data/runs/2026-02-25/verdicts/keep/idea_0018.json__0001.json`

## 3) How will they hear about it? (Free channels only)
- Cold email to sales ops / founders of SMB SaaS or service firms
- Partnerships with CRM consultants (HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce SMB)
- LinkedIn posts focused on “missed renewals cost”

## 4) How will they try it?
- Free setup: upload CSV of contracts, get reminder schedule
- 7–14 day trial with email reminders + basic dashboard
- Optional: “renewal audit” PDF for first 50 signups

## 5) How will they pay you?
- Simple tiers based on #contracts
- Example: $29/mo (<=50), $59/mo (<=200), $99/mo (<=500)
- Annual option: 2 months free

## Cheap execution checklist
- Landing page + 60-second demo video
- CSV upload + email reminders
- Stripe checkout
- Simple CRM integration later (phase 2)

## Automation / simplification ideas
- Auto-detect renewal dates from emailed PDFs (later)
- One-click “add to calendar” button
- Weekly renewal digest email
